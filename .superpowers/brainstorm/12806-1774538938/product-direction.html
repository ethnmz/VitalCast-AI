What should VitalCast's "wow" moment be?

Each direction gives judges a completely different reaction. Pick the one that excites you most — or mix elements.

A

📷 Real-Time Camera Vitals

The pitch: "No file needed. Just look at your camera."

Use rPPG (photoplethysmography) — the same tech in smartwatches — to measure your heart rate live from your face via webcam. Heart rate appears in real-time, feeds into the Framingham model instantly, CVD risk score populates live. Zero setup. Judges just hold up their phone.

✦ Most "magic" demo moment  ·  Technically impressive  ·  No file upload required
B

🎙️ Talk To Your Health Data

The pitch: "Ask Vita anything about your body."

Voice-enabled AI conversation powered by Claude. Upload once, then ask: "Why did my stress spike Tuesday?" "What happens to my heart risk if I sleep 8 hours?" "Compare my last 3 weeks." Vita responds with voice + visual charts. Turns a dashboard into a dialogue.

✦ Showcases Claude deeply  ·  Feels futuristic  ·  High emotional resonance
C

🎛️ "What If" Health Simulator

The pitch: "Watch your future risk change in real time."

Animated live simulator: drag sliders for sleep, exercise, stress, smoking — watch the 30-day risk trajectory animate and recalculate instantly. Show the compounding effect of small daily habits over months. "If you sleep 1 more hour a night, your CVD risk drops 3.2pp in 90 days."

✦ Instantly interactive  ·  Clear practical value  ·  Judges can play with it live
D

📈 Your Health Story Over Time

The pitch: "The first app that shows where your health has been going for years."

Upload multiple Garmin/Apple Health exports (monthly, quarterly). VitalCast builds a longitudinal health timeline — "Your HRV dropped 18% in March when you started a new job." "Your CVD risk has improved steadily since you started running in September." Vita narrates your health biography.

✦ Deeply personal  ·  No competitor does this  ·  Strong narrative angle
