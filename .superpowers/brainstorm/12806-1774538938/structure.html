How should the experience be structured after upload?

A

🎬 Three-Act Journey

Three full screens, each with one clear purpose

🔍
Act 1
"Here's what I found"
Vita diagnoses your top 3 risks, ranked by severity. Clinical findings with real model names.
📈
Act 2
"Here's your future"
The simulator. Two paths side by side — do nothing vs. make one change. Watch the curves diverge.
✅
Act 3
"Here's your plan"
One specific weekly goal from Vita. Evidence for why this lever matters most. Option to set a reminder.
✦ Cleanest demo to present  ·  Each screen has emotional beat  ·  Judges follow a story
B

🧭 Vita-Guided Tour

One dashboard, Vita walks you through it step by step

Dashboard loads → Vita highlights the #1 problem with a spotlight effect
→ Speech bubble: "Let's start here — your HRV stress index"
→ Click "Tell me more" → panel expands with clinical detail + simulator
→ Vita moves to next finding: "Now look at this..."
→ User can explore freely or follow Vita's guided path
→ Ends with a "Your action plan" section at the bottom
✦ Feels like a tour guide  ·  User stays on one screen  ·  Familiar dashboard feel
C

📋 The Health Report

Long-scroll single page — like a real clinical report

Top: Vita's opening summary paragraph (warm, encouraging)
Section 1: Clinical Findings — ranked risk factors with severity badges
Section 2: What This Means Long-Term — simulator embedded inline
Section 3: Model Evidence — equations, confidence intervals, feature weights
Section 4: Your Action Plan — specific weekly goals, expected outcomes
Footer: "Share this report" / "Re-scan in 2 weeks"
✦ Most technically impressive visually  ·  Shareable  ·  Feels like a real medical doc
